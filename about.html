how to place order online?

If you want to place an order, you proceed the following steps: Step 1 Place the products in the shopping cart. The prices shown on the website are inclusive of VAT. Step 2 As soon as you have added the products, go to the shopping cart and choose "continue with checkout". Step 3 You will now be taken to a new screen where you can enter your contact details. This is done in a safe environment. Go through the 4 steps here: - Step 1: Enter your address details. - Step 2: Choose a shipping method - Step 3: Choose a payment method - Step 4: Check your order. Forgot something? Then choose the link "Go back to your shopping cart". When you agree to the order and the general terms and conditions, tick "I agree to the general terms and conditions" and press the button "place order". Make sure you can cancel your order up to the last moment. Afterwards you can still make use of the right of withdrawal. We advise you to contact As soon as your order has been placed we will do everything we can to deliver your goods as soon as possible.

is online payment safe?

Online transactions are more popular and secure than ever before, thanks to advancements in digital payments technology, demographic shifts, and the evolving cyber-security landscape. At the same time, offline payments seem more insecure than ever before. The outbreak of high-profile security breaches at major retailers has shed light on the fact that offline transactions are vulnerable to attack.
